Who
am I?

Born and raised in Toms River, New Jersey, I attended the Marine Academy of Technology and Environmental Science for high school. I now attend Rochester Institute of Technology, specifically Saunders College of Business — an AACSB-accredited program.

Global Business Management Management Information Systems Marketing (minor) Event Management (minor)

I'm majoring in Global Business Management because I've always been curious about cultures around the world. I studied abroad in Zagreb, Croatia, from January to May 2025, growing in an entirely new environment. I love leading teams — from serving as team captain on my soccer team to directing peer mentorship within the Leadership Academy.

My second major, Management Information Systems, came after a Digital Marketing internship that revealed how much I love the intersection of technology and business. I enjoy finding patterns and turning them into something useful, connecting creative problem-solving with real data.

My goal is to build a career that lets me travel, lead teams, and solve business problems through a mix of marketing, strategy, and innovation.
